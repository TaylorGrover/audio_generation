Sequence instrument signals with pitch and duration over a timeline.

* The sequencer interface should have a dropdown for selected instruments
* The ability to set snap-grid intervals (e.g., triplets, quarter, eighth,
  sixteenth, thirty-second, 1/9th)
* Pitch selection for each item. Pitch and time axes can flip. Pitch can
  increase from south to north while time travels left to right, or vice
  versa.
